Safety & Compliance

Our commitments to safety, rights protection, and regulatory compliance.

Our Safety Commitments

Rights Protections

We are committed to protecting and promoting the rights of individuals we serve. This includes rights to dignity, privacy, choice, and participation in decisions about their services and daily lives.

Incident Reporting

We maintain clear procedures for reporting and responding to incidents. Caregivers are trained to recognize reportable events and follow established protocols for documentation and notification.

Grievance Pathways

Individuals, families, and caregivers have access to processes for raising concerns or complaints. We take all concerns seriously and work toward fair resolution.

Abuse & Neglect Prevention

Prevention of abuse, neglect, and exploitation is central to our work. Caregivers are screened, trained, and monitored. All team members understand their obligation to report suspected mistreatment.

Emergency Preparedness

Host homes maintain emergency plans and caregivers are trained in emergency response. Plans address various scenarios including medical emergencies, natural disasters, and other urgent situations.

Confidentiality & Privacy

We maintain HIPAA-aligned privacy practices where applicable. Personal information is protected and shared only as needed for service delivery or as required by law.

Regulatory Compliance

Care for Life Home Services operates in accordance with applicable New Hampshire state regulations, waiver requirements, and provider standards. We participate in required oversight activities and work to maintain compliance with all applicable rules and guidelines.

Our policies and procedures are designed to support both regulatory compliance and the delivery of quality, person-centered services.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have questions about our safety practices or wish to raise a concern, please contact us.
