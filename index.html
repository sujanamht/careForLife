About Us

Personalized Care That Empowers

Care for Life Home Services, LLC is a New Hampshire–based human services organization dedicated to providing high-quality, person-centered care for adults with developmental disabilities. Our approach is rooted in respect, compassion, and dignity.

Learn About Us

Support at every step

We deliver individualized supports tailored to each person's strengths, preferences, and goals. Through Enhanced Family Care (EFC) and Host Home services under New Hampshire's Developmental Disabilities (DD) Waiver, we create safe, stable, family-based living environments.

How It Works

Supporting Independence & Inclusion

Our commitment goes beyond daily care. We foster independence, prioritize health and safety, and encourage meaningful community participation—ensuring every individual feels supported, included, and valued.
