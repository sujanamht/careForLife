Who We Serve

Information about eligibility and the individuals we support.

Eligibility

Care for Life Home Services provides Enhanced Family Care (EFC) / Host Home services for adults with developmental disabilities who are eligible for services under New Hampshire's Home and Community-Based Services (HCBS) Developmental Disabilities (DD) Waiver.

Eligibility for waiver services is determined by the state. Individuals interested in services should work with their Area Agency or service coordinator to explore options and determine eligibility.

Service Focus

Our services focus on supporting individuals in community-based living arrangements. We work with adults who:

  • Are 18 years of age or older
  • Have a developmental disability as defined by state eligibility criteria
  • Are eligible for NH HCBS DD Waiver services
  • Are seeking residential supports in a host home / shared living arrangement

Community-Based Living

Our services emphasize community inclusion and participation. Individuals we support live in host homes located throughout New Hampshire communities, with access to:

Local Resources

Access to community services, shops, recreation, and amenities.

Social Connections

Opportunities for relationships and community involvement.

Individualized Support

Assistance tailored to personal needs and preferences.

Home Environment

Living as part of a household rather than in a facility setting.
