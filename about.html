About Us

Care for Life Home Services, LLC

Care for Life Home Services, LLC is a New Hampshire-based human services organization established to provide high-quality, person-centered supports to adults with developmental disabilities through home- and community-based service models.

The organization's primary and current service focus is the delivery of Enhanced Family Care (EFC) / Host Home services under New Hampshire's Developmental Disabilities (DD) Waiver. Care for Life Home Services, LLC is committed to supporting individuals in safe, stable, family-based living environments that promote independence, dignity, and community inclusion.

Our Mission

To support individuals with developmental disabilities in living within their communities with dignity, choice, and the opportunity for meaningful participation in daily life.
