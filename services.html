Service Model

Enhanced Family Care (EFC) /
Host Home

Care for Life Home Services, LLC provides Enhanced Family Care (EFC) services in which individuals reside in private family homes and receive individualized supports from trained host families.

Key Elements of the EFC Model

  • Placement in stable, family-based living environments
  • Individualized service planning based on assessed needs
  • Ongoing oversight, monitoring, and support by the provider
  • Emphasis on community integration, skill development, and natural supports

Our Responsibilities

  • Recruiting, screening, and approving host families
  • Providing required training and ongoing support to host families
  • Monitoring service delivery and individual wellbeing
  • Coordinating with care managers, guardians, and other providers
  • Ensuring compliance with all EFC program standards and regulations
